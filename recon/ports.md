# Ports

## General
- `53`: DNS.
- `88`: Kerberos.

## Windows
- `464, 3268`: [GlobalCatalog](https://theitbros.com/global-catalog-active-directory/).
- `389`: LDAP.
- `636`: LDAPS (LDAP over SSL).
- `135`: Microsoft Windows RPC.
- `139`: SMB over NetBIOS.
- `445`: SMB over TCP.
- `593`: RPC over HTTP.
